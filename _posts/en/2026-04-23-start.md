# The First Step

I've been thinking for a while about whether I wanted to start a blog. I thought: *"I don't even know anything that I could explain to others."* But then I learned that a blog for programer's doesn't mean i have to be a professor. It's about documenting my own progress and projects. Primarily for myself, secondarily for the rest of the world!

Additionally, yesterday on April 22, 2026, [Felix Klinge](https://felixk15.github.io) visited my campus at SAE Institute Cologne. He inspired me to finally make this decision!

I also initially oriented the theme of this blog towards his, though I'm not sure if it will stay that way. Actually, I’d love to build a site for this myself at some point, maybe even away from GitHub (Fuck Microslop) :D

## What you will find here
In the future, I will record things here that occupy me during my studies or while programming:
* Fixed bugs
* Documentation for projects (both within the scope of my Game Programming studies and personal projects)
* Small tutorials for my future self & you

## The Setup
This blog runs on the [**Chirpy Jekyll Theme**](https://github.com/cotes2020/jekyll-theme-chirpy) on GitHub Pages. Why? Because it looks damn good and because [Felix Klinge](https://felixk15.github.io) uses it too.

That's it for the beginning. The foundation has been laid!
